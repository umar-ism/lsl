Education

IELTS

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is the common teaching method. In LSL, IELTS consists of 6 stages. Every stages is about 2 months.

CEFR

CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) is very popular in Uzbekistan. In our education center CEFR has 3 stages. Every stages is about 3 months.

Maths

This subject is for students who wants to take ---. It hasn't stages at all. Mathematics is taught individually for each pupil. It takes an average of 6 months to learn Maths.
